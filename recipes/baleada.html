Baleada Recipe

Description

The Baleada, originated from the country of Honduras, consist of

a flour tortilla, smashed beans and cream.

Some adds on are eggs, sweat plantains and avocado

ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Smashed beans
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Heat the flour tortilla on a pan at medium heat
  2. Once the tortilla is ready add the beans
  3. Put the cream over the beans
  4. Fold the tortilla on half
  5. Enjoy!
